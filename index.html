Your 5 Rules
01
☕ Chai Allowed — Biscuits Controlled (Max 2)
Don't eliminate your chai ritual — it's yours. Just upgrade what goes with it. Max 2 biscuits, then reach for roasted chana or makhana.
02
🥩 Protein in EVERY Meal — No Skipping
Dal, eggs, chicken, paneer, curd, whey — every single meal needs a protein source. This one rule alone will change how your body looks.
03
🚌 No Random Boredom Eating on the Bus
2 hours of travel is prime boredom-snacking time. Use that time to plan your meals, track food, or listen to music. If genuinely hungry, bring a portion-controlled snack from home.
04
🏋️ Gym 6 Days = Non-Negotiable
You said 6 days. That's the commitment. Miss one occasionally — life happens. But "I'll go tomorrow" is a trap. Your future body is built by today's you.
05
😴 Sleep Must Reach 7+ Hours
This will change your body faster than almost anything else. Sleep is when fat loss and muscle growth actually happen. 5.5 hours is sabotaging your 6-day gym effort. Be in bed by 10:30pm.
